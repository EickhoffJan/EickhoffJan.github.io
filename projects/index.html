Seminars

  • Homoclinic Points and Period Doubling Chaotic dynamics, TUM (Apr 2023)
    An introduction to homoclinic points and their connection to chaotic dynamics – defining the concept, exploring their link to homoclinic orbits, and highlighting their role in the emergence of chaos.
  • Classification of Isometries in the Euclidean Plane Geometry and Groups, Hurwitz Seminar - TUM (Sep 2024)
    A concise overview of all distance‐preserving maps in the plane – translations, rotations, reflections and glide reflections – showing each arises from at most three reflections and forming the foundation for higher‐dimensional generalizations.
  • Rational Functions on Plane Curves Algebraic Curves, TUM (Jun 2025)
    The field of rational functions on projective plane curves over an algebraically closed field was investigated, including the reconstruction of the one‑variable function field, its realization in the projective plane, decomposition into components and birational classification.
  • TBA Category Theory by Examples, TUM (Oct 2026)
    The seminar is ongoing.
