Seminars

  • Homoclinic points and period doubling Chaotic dynamics, TUM
    An introduction to homoclinic points and their connection to chaotic dynamics—defining the concept, exploring their link to homoclinic orbits, and highlighting their role in the emergence of chaos.
  • Classification of isometries in the Euclidean plane Geometry and Groups, Hurwitz Seminar - TUM
    A concise overview of all distance‐preserving maps in the plane—translations, rotations, reflections and glide reflections—showing each arises from at most three reflections and forming the foundation for higher‐dimensional generalizations.
  • Rational functions on plane curves Algebraic Curves, TUM
    This seminar is ongoing.
